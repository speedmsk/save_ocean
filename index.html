How it works

Your step-by-step guide to saving our oceans

01

Make mindful choices (every action counts)

Small changes lead to big results. Choose reusable items, avoid single-use plastics, and make ocean-friendly purchases. Every decision you make helps keep our oceans clean and full of life for generations to come

02

Take Action for Cleaner Oceans

Once per week, join an ocean-friendly initiative to reduce waste and pollution. You can participate in beach cleanups, support sustainable seafood choices, or even start your own awareness campaign. Every small step makes a big difference for our oceans.

03

Take Action for Cleaner Oceans at Convenient Times

Join local clean-up events organized by ocean conservation groups. Participate at times that fit your schedule, helping make a difference when it's most convenient for you.
